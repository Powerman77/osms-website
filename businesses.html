01 / Business Line

Scrap Management
& Metal Trading

Certified collection, segregation and disposal of industrial and residential scrap, combined with large-scale ferrous and non-ferrous metal trading — buying directly from manufacturers, utilities and Defence depots, and from Government of India e-auctions through our registration as an approved buyer on MSTC Limited. Every lot is weighbridge-verified and documented, giving institutional sellers a clean, auditable chain of custody from collection through to final sale.

Services include:

  • · E-waste recycling services
  • · Office & building/structure dismantling
  • · Old engineering machinery scrap handling
  • · All kinds of cable & wire dismantling

06 / Business Line

Plastic Waste
Management

Registered with the Rajasthan State Pollution Control Board for recycling and processing of plastic waste under the Plastic Waste Management Rules, 2016 — supporting EPR compliance for producers, importers and brand owners.

Why it matters: recycling reduces the need for raw materials, cuts energy use, and lowers pollution — recycled production can use significantly less water and energy than manufacturing from scratch.

Registering authorityRajasthan Pollution Control Board
Processing codesR1–R6, E1–E4
Processing capacity1,800 TPA (Cat-I & Cat-II)
Output productRecycled PP / LDPE, 1,800 TPA
Standard followedIS 14534:1998
