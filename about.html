About OSMS Group

Who We Are

Two decades
in the yard.

OSMS Group has been in the scrap and industrial materials business since 2001. What began as a focused scrap trading operation has grown into a professionally managed group offering certified scrap disposal, metal and machinery trading, plastic waste recycling, transportation, manpower and construction services to corporate, industrial and government clients across India.

Our flagship operating entity, OmTraders Scrap Management Services Pvt. Ltd., is registered with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, the Rajasthan Pollution Control Board, and is an approved buyer on MSTC Limited, the Government of India's e-auction platform — giving every client a transparent, traceable chain of custody for their materials.

Our Philosophy

The 5S discipline.

OSMS Group and its operations are built on the adoption and implementation of the 5S principles. These aren't a poster on the wall — they're how every yard, warehouse and site actually runs.

1S Sort Tidiness and organization. We sort through all tools and materials in our work area and keep only essential items — everything else is stored or discarded. Fewer hazards and less clutter have increased our productivity.
2S Straighten Orderliness. Our focus is on an orderly workplace — tools and equipment are arranged in an order that promotes workflow and eliminates extra motion.
3S Shine Systemized cleanliness. We keep our workplace clean and neat at all times, because maintaining cleanliness is part of our daily work, not an exception to it.
4S Standardize Standardized work practices. We operate in a consistent fashion across sites, and everyone knows exactly what their responsibilities are.
5S Sustain Sustaining discipline. We aim to maintain these standards as our permanent way of operating — we do not tolerate a gradual decline back to old habits.
